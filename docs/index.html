Description

Difficulty in breathing caused by obstruction of Prana Vayu in the respiratory channels. Ayurveda describes five types with varying severity.

🏥 Bronchial Asthma / Dyspnea (ICD-10: J45, R06.0)
🔍
Root Causes (Nidāna)
Exposure to cold and wind Dust and smoke inhalation Excessive cold food/drinks Suppression of natural urges Chest injuries Kapha-aggravating diet
🩺
Symptoms (Rūpa)
Difficulty breathing Wheezing sound Chest tightness Cough with sputum Cannot speak long sentences Restlessness

Pathya — Diet

✅ Foods to Favor

Warm light food Old rice Ginger tea Honey Garlic Turmeric milk Pepper

❌ Foods to Avoid

Cold food & drinks Curd Banana Heavy foods Oily/fried food Black gram
💡 Eat warm, light, freshly cooked food. Avoid eating to full capacity. Dinner should be early and light.
🏃
Lifestyle (Vihāra)
  • Avoid cold exposure
  • Keep chest warm
  • Avoid dust and smoke
  • Sleep with head elevated
  • Avoid daytime sleep

Prognosis
